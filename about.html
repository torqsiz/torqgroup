About

What TORQ Is

We are not a supplier. We manage sourcing and coordination for operational businesses.

Operational businesses — construction, logistics, industrial, facilities — have a consistent problem: getting the right materials, at the right price, at the right time.

The procurement process gets in the way. Chasing suppliers. Inconsistent pricing. Delivery that doesn't show. Availability that changes. These issues take up time that should go into running the operation.

TORQ exists to handle that coordination. We sit between the operation and the supply chain — managing sourcing, pricing, and delivery so the operation keeps moving.

What We Are

A procurement and supply coordination business.

What We Are Not

A supplier. We don't hold stock. We manage the sourcing process.

Where We Operate

Johannesburg, South Africa. Available nationally for sourcing and coordination.

Where We Step In

Sourcing Delays

When your team is spending time sourcing instead of operating. We take that off the plate.

Pricing Inconsistency

When suppliers quote differently every time and you can't benchmark. We structure pricing so you can compare.

Supplier Reliability

When delivery doesn't arrive on time or specs don't match. We manage the supplier accountability so you don't have to.
